About This Website

This model provides an overview of the property prices in various cities across India. It aims to offer prospective buyers an idea of the average cost of properties in different parts of the country. The prices mentioned here are based on a combination of data collected from various sources, including real estate websites, kaggle, and other relevant sources. It provides a comprehensive overview of property prices in different cities. It is important to note that the prices mentioned here are subject to change, and the actual cost of properties may vary based on a range of factors, including location, demand, supply, and other factors. Overall, our model is designed to be a Project for Personal. We did not take responsibility of the Price shown here because the sources used for prediction can be wrong.
